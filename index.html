Private Family Drivers

Corporate & Residential Security Guards

Disciplined individuals selected for alert monitoring, residential gatekeeping, and corporate property protection.

Gardeners & Agricultural Workers

General Cleaning & Hospitality Staff

Regulatory & Safety Alignment

CoC Certification

Every domestic worker (both male and female) is fully verified through the Certificate of Competency (CoC) via the Ethiopian Ministry of Labour and Skills before application matching.

GCC License Verification

All male private drivers undergo strict background and physical driving tests to verify their capability before their profiles are uploaded onto Musaned or submitted to Kuwaiti offices.

Bilateral Contract Protection

All jobs strictly adhere to official minimum wage standards and mandatory insurance guidelines set by the respective embassies.
